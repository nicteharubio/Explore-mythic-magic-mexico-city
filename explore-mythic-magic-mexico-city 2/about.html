Hi! My name is Nicteha Rubio, a passionate web developer and traveler. My love for Mexico City inspired me to share its wonders online. From pre-Columbian museums to delicious food and historic neighborhoods, there's always something new to explore.

I'm especially drawn to the city's vibrant art scene and its unique role as a crossroads of history. Creating this site allowed me to merge my interest in code with my admiration for Mexican culture.
